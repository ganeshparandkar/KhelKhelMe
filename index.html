Our Story

Khel Khel Mein is an organisation founded by Game Therapist and Counsellor Mital Salia, with the purpose to develop Life Skills and Critical Thinking Skills through Games and Play. With close to 2 decades of experience in the field of transformational education, Mital has successfully transformed herself into a Game Therapist and a parallel educator. It is her undying passion in bringing out the best meaning out of existing games and play, and using them for thinking development, Socio-emotional development and improving the adversity quotient. Her background of Counselling Psychology, along with her diploma in ‘Experiential Education’ and NLP and Hypnotherapy has contributed to her abilities to become a better educator. Mital has worked with all the top educational companies & has been instrumental in product development with her new innovative ideas in each of the companies.
She has served in companies such as Kidzee, Educomp, Mexus Education, Naandi Foundation, etc. in various capacities ranging from content and holistic product development to Teacher Training Head. Having seen the educational scenario so closely, she decided to quit her job and work on developing those life skills that often go missing in school education.
She began with teaching Creative Writing to children to focus on ‘Communicative English Skills’ through games. The main feature of every session was half an hour of Game Time. She simultaneously began with Game classes for children with Games, Toys and Puzzles that enhance one’s thinking skills. Her passion towards understanding games kept growing and she has gone into the depth of the Psychology and Neurology of games, and how each game play affects the different lobes of the brain OR affects the performance of different functions of the brain and mind. Mital has also travelled to multiple places across India to understand and gather a collection of ‘Ancient Board Games’. Khel Khel Mein runs a club for children to sharpen their strategic thinking skills through this ancient wisdom. Currently her team at Khel Khel Mein is actively engaged in conducting Treasure Hunts and Game-a-thons for all ages; they run Game-Based classes round the year for children and also conduct various trainer-level workshops. They are soon venturing into Corporates and Schools with game-based solutions for the Industry 4.0 skills.
